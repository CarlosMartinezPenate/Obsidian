## **Purpose**
Receive the incoming axenic dinoflagellate strains safely, minimize stress and contamination, and set up a simple **IMK vs F/2** comparison during the first 1–2 weeks. We expect 5 tubes (1 for strain) with 5ml of liquid algal biomass (approximately 5 million cells per culture).
## **Incoming strains - specific culture notes**
From: [[xiang2013]]
Expected:
- *Symbiodinium*: *linucheae* (SSA01), *necroappetens* (SSA02), *pilosum* (SSA03) 
	- **SSA02**:
		- grew very slowly in IMK alone or with only one supplement
		- Robust growth occurred only in **IMK + casein + glucose**
		- no growth in the dark
		- appeared **light-sensitive**, with better growth at lower light
- *Breviolum minutum* (SSB01)
	- - **SSB01**:
		- Could grow photoautotrophically, heterotrophically, or mixotrophically
		- Fixed carbon, especially **glucose**, increased growth
		- Glucose also caused a marked drop in normal chlorophyll fluorescence, consistent with pigment loss / bleaching-like behavior
- *Effrenium voratum* (SSE01)
	- **SSE01**:
		- Grew well in **IMK**
		- Also in **IMK + glucose**
		- Growth was **depressed by casein hydrolysate**
		- Did **not** grow in the dark with glucose or casein
		- Interpreted as an **obligate photoautotroph** under those tested conditions
Expected maintenance conditions:
- liquid IMK liquid medium at  24–26°C in ~15 lmol photons m 2 s 1 light.

## Parent Culture Reserve
### **Initial clonal isolation assurance**
Goal, to make sure the parent axenic cultures we've received are free of contaminants prior to its long term keeping (plated cultures)
### Step 1: Plate cells
- Spread culture onto **solid F/2 medium (agar)**
- Incubate:
    - 24–26°C
    - 12h light / 12h dark
    - ~30 µmol photons m⁻² s⁻¹  
    - Wait **2–4 weeks** for colonies to form
---
### *Step 2: Colony purification
- Repeat this cycle:
	- Pick a **single colony**
	- Streak onto fresh plate
	- Grow again
	 - Do this multiple rounds to ensure clonality
---
### *Step 3: Antibiotic selection*
- Transfer colonies to **F/2 + KAS antibiotics**:
    - kanamycin (50 µg/mL)
    - ampicillin (100 µg/mL)
    - streptomycin (50 µg/mL)
- Repeat streaking several times
This progressively removes bacteria
---
### *Step 4: Re-isolate single colonies again
- After antibiotic treatment:
    - Pick a **single colony again**
    - Restreak on antibiotic plates
    - Ensures clonal and axenic
---
### **Long-term parent culture maintenance**
### Step 5: Routine maintenance

- Initially:
    - Maintain on **F/2 + antibiotics plates**
    - Passage ~**monthly**
- Later (once clean):
    - Switch to an **antibiotic-free medium**
    - Continue **monthly passaging**
    - Routinely check for clonal isolation
