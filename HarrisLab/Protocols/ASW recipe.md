Prepare liquid media

- 1 Artificial seawater (ASW) is the base for most of the media, except Marine Broth.

Making ASW

- 2 Dissolve 37.1 g [[Coral Pro]] salt into 1L pure water; check the salinity of the dissolved seawater and make sure it is between 32 and 34.

Note: This step of dissolving sea salts may take a while, stir overnight is recommended.
