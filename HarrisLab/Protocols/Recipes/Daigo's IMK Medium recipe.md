
Starting with [[10x Daigo's IMK stock recipe]]:
or with [[50x Daigo's IMK Stock]] properly diluted provided by Ting Ting

1. In a sterile hood
2. Transfer 90 mL filtered sterilized [[ASW]] into a 250 mL PYREX Glass Erlenmeyer Flask, and cover with aluminum foil;
3. Add 10 mL Daigo's IMK Medium stock solution to the flask; and that makes 100 mL IMK.
