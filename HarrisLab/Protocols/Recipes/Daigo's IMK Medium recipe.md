
Starting with [[10x Daigo's IMK stock recipe]]:

1. Transfer 90 mL [[ASW]] into a 250 mL PYREX Glass Erlenmeyer Flask, and cover with aluminum foil;
2. Autoclave at 121 ºC for 30 minutes;
3.  Let it cool to room temperature;
4. Bring the flask from step 1 and [[10x Daigo's IMK stock recipe]] solution to a sterile hood;
5. Add 10 mL Daigo's IMK Medium stock solution to the flask; and that makes 100 mL IMK.
