1. Dissolve 0.5 g glucose into 90 mL ASW, and cover with aluminum foil;
2. Autoclave at 121 ºC for 30 minutes;
3. Let it cool to room temperature.
4. Bring the flask from step 17 and 10X Daigo's IMK Medium stock solution from to a sterile hood;
5. Add 10 mL Daigo stock solution to the flask; and that makes 100 mL IMK+Glc medium.
