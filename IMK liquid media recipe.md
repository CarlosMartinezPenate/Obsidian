# 10x Daigo stock solution recipe
3 Dissolve 2.56g Daigo's IMK Medium powder into 1L ASW;

# Making 10x Daigo's IMK Medium stock solution
4 Filter sterile the 10X Daigo's IMK Medium solution through a 0.22 µm filter;

# Making liquid IMK medium
5 Transfer 90 mL ASW into a 250 mL PYREX Glass Erlenmeyer Flask, and cover with aluminum foil;
6 Autoclave at 121 ºC for 30 minutes;
7 Let it cool to room temperature.
8 Bring the flask from step 7 and 10X Daigo's IMK Medium stock solution from step 4 to a sterile hood;
9 Add 10 mL Daigo's IMK Medium stock solution to the flask; and that makes 100 mL IMK.
