> Solid ammonium sulfate was added to the resulting supernatant to achieve 70% saturation, followed by centrifugation at 8000 g for 15 min. The resulting supernatant was dialyzed against 20 μM Tris–HCl (pH 8.0), concentrated, filtered through a 0.2 μm filter, and applied to a HiLoadTM SuperdexTM 200 prep grade column. The PCP fraction eluted by 20 mM TrisHCl (pH 8.0), was then applied to a HiTrapTM Q SepharoseTM HP column and eluted with a linear gradient of NaCl from 0 to 0.5 M in the same buffer. SDS-PAGE was performed to confirm the size and purity of PCP [p. 3](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/NNNQB83I?page=3)
